Comparaison between the Models metrics

In our analysis, we meticulously evaluated the performance of three distinct models using four critical metrics: accuracy, negative binomial (nb), F1 score, and recall. By leveraging the power of visualization, we aimed to comprehensively compare the efficacy of these models across each metric. We first extracted the relevant performance data from each model, encapsulated within pickle files. These metrics were then consolidated into a single dataset, enabling us to create insightful visual representations.
